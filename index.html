Ingrediends

Graham Cracker Crust

1 and 1/2 cups (150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)

5 Tablespoons (70g) unsalted butter,melted

1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

Cheescake

four 8-ounce blocks (904g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature

1 cup (200g) granulated sugar

1 cup (204g) full-fat sour cream, at room temperature

1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)

3 large eggs, at room temperature

topping suggestion: salted caramel, lemon curs, strawberry topping, chocolate ganache, red wine chocolate ganache, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or raspberry sauce(recipe in notes)
